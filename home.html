Who We Are

The Nigerian Airspace Management Agency (NAMA) is the sole air navigation service
provider in Nigeria. We are responsible for providing safe, effective and economical air
navigation services to all airspace users, both civil and military. Our mission is to
transform the Nigerian airspace into one of the safest in the world
